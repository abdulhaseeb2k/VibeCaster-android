# VibeCaster v1.0 Release Notes

Welcome to the official release of **VibeCaster**! 

VibeCaster is a high-performance 8D Music Player that allows you to experience your favorite YouTube and local tracks with immersive spatial audio effects.

## 🚀 Key Features
- **YouTube Integration**: Search and stream any song directly from YouTube.
- **8D Audio Engine**: Real-time 8D spatialization with adjustable rotation speed and intensity.
- **Offline Mode**: Download your favorite YouTube tracks for offline playback.
- **Audius Support**: Discover and stream tracks from the Audius decentralized music platform.
- **Custom Playlists**: Create and manage your own music collections.
- **Dynamic Themes**: Choose between Vibe, Dark, and Light modes to suit your style.
- **Export to Music**: Easily move your downloaded tracks to your public Music folder.

## 🛠️ Improvements & Fixes
- Fixed "Unresolved reference" issues in the UI components.
- Streamlined the app startup by removing the mandatory login gate.
- Optimized YouTube stream resolution for faster loading.
- Improved background playback stability.

## 📦 Installation
1. Download the `app-release.apk` from the assets below.
2. Open the APK on your Android device (Android 12+ recommended).
3. If prompted, allow installation from "Unknown Sources".
4. Open VibeCaster and start casting your vibes!
